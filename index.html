Who We Are

More than a café —
a community table

Tucked on Mutungo Hill Road in Kampala, The Bend 25 Café was born from a love of good food and even better conversations. We built this space to be a true neighbourhood cornerstone — somewhere you can start your morning, celebrate a birthday, or wind down with friends as the sun sets over the hills.

Our team takes pride in every plate, every cup, and every warm welcome from the stairs. From our diverse menu of international favourites to proud local classics, we pour Ugandan heart into everything we do.
